Service Center Sign-Up Page

Thank you for your interest in our Ignition Interlock Service Center Program. For your convenience, we provide a simple process to electronically approve the agreements that you received via email. Please follow the simple steps below:

  • Verify the Information that we have on-file for your service center is accurate and up to date.
  • Schedule a time and date for on-site visit from a Budget IID Certified Field Representative
  • Review the terms and conditions of the Serivce Agreement, ensure that your facility is compliant with all requirements
  • Press the 'I Agree' button - and you're done.

Once you've reviewed the agreements that were sent to you via email, this entire process should take you approximately 3-5 minutes.
