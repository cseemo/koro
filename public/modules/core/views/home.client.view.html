Welcome to the Koro Konnections IP Demo Portal

This portal is designed to be a simple demonstration of the Koro Konnection IP Monitoring platform. The cloud based environment is designed to provide a central platform for IP Camera monitoring at multiple facilities.

  • View Feed By Location
  • Receive SMS/Email Notifications
  • Watch Live Camera Feeds
  • Watch Previously Recorded Content
  • Remotely Control IP Cameras (Pan, Tilt, Zoom)
  • Two-Way Audio Between Cameras and Users
