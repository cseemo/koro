Welcome to the Budget IID Online Portal

This portal is designed to be your go-to source for information on Budget Ignition Interlock as well as the way that you will log, track, and verify all of your customer appointments, orders, and payments.

This portal is designed to streamline the process and simplify each step along the way, including:

  • Automatically Sends Calendar Reminders for Upcoming Appointments
  • Electronic Signature for Work Authorizations
  • Automatic Credit Card/Debit Card Payments
  • Work Order & Billing History
